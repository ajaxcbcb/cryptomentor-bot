Capital Protection

Risk management isn't optional — it's the edge.

  • Risk defined as a fixed % of equity per trade — never a raw dollar amount
  • Stop loss is placed before position size is calculated
  • Max concurrent exposure capped across all open positions
  • Correlated pairs monitored to avoid stacked directional risk
  • Leverage used as a tool, not a goal — effective exposure stays bounded
  • Drawdown thresholds trigger automatic reduction in activity
With controlled sizing and predefined invalidation, liquidation risk approaches zero under normal execution conditions. No strategy removes risk entirely.
